About

Researcher and educator with deep expertise in artificial intelligence, deep learning, and machine learning applied to communication engineering — specialising in 5G/B5G, MIMO mmWave, Visible Light Communication, Metaverse and Digital Twin technologies.

I build innovative deep‑learning models and ML solutions (SVM‑based detection, CNN‑based channel estimation) for the practical problems operators and infrastructure vendors face: noisy channels, low-SNR estimation, autonomous-vehicle perception, and efficient hybrid precoding for massive MIMO.

With 15+ peer-reviewed publications, hands-on experience in live 5G field trials (Huawei, Chulalongkorn Sandbox), and collaboration history spanning regulators, operators and equipment vendors across Asia and Europe, I am now looking to translate that depth into measurable network and customer-experience gains in industry.

Research Interests

AI & Deep Learning for Communication Systems

CNN, MLP and Residual networks for channel estimation, signal recovery and joint estimation tasks.

ML for Signal Processing

SVM-based detection, subspace methods, and statistical-learning approaches for low-SNR regimes.

5G & Beyond 5G (B5G)

OFDM systems, 6 GHz IMT band, network optimisation and spectrum planning for next-gen mobile.

Direction-of-Arrival Estimation

Covariance-matrix reconstruction and subspace methods to improve DoA accuracy under low SNR.

MIMO mmWave Communication

Hybrid precoding, massive MIMO downlink, and SSOR-based low-complexity precoding schemes.

Visible Light Communication

Joint position and orientation estimation for VLC positioning using deep-learning approaches.

Metaverse & Digital Twins

AI-driven digital twins and intelligent automation control frameworks for smart-environment applications.
